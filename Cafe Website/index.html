Our Menu

  • Espresso

    A bold and rich single shot of pure coffee with a smooth crema on top. Small in size, big in flavor.

  • Americano

    Espresso blended with hot water for a smooth, balanced, and classic black coffee experience.

  • Cappuccino

    A perfect harmony of espresso, steamed milk, and thick foam. Creamy, light, and comforting.

  • Latte

    Smooth espresso mixed with silky steamed milk and a light layer of foam. Soft, creamy, and easy to love.

  • Flat White

    Stronger than a latte, with velvety microfoam and a rich espresso flavor.

  • Mocha

    Espresso with chocolate and steamed milk, topped with foam. Sweet, rich, and indulgent.

  • Cup Cake

    A soft, fluffy treat topped with creamy frosting, baked fresh to bring a sweet smile in every bite.

  • Burger

    A juicy, flavorful burger made with fresh ingredients and served in a soft bun for the perfect satisfying bite.

  • Sandwich

  • Ice Cream

    Creamy, smooth, and delightfully cold, available in delicious flavors to refresh and sweeten your day.

  • Orange Juice
